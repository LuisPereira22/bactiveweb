About the Workshop

Our workshop is a friendly space designed specifically for seniors who want to embrace active living. We believe that health is not just about the body, but also about nutrition, connection, and joy.

Whether you are looking to improve your diet, find simple ways to stay moving, or just want to meet like-minded neighbors, you are warmly welcome here.
